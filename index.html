Fun Facts About South Korea

  • South Korea is home to the world’s fastest internet connection.
  • The country has a high density of cafes, with unique themes and atmospheres.
  • Korean pop culture, including K-Pop and K-Dramas, has gained immense global popularity.
  • Kimchi, a traditional fermented vegetable dish, is a staple in Korean cuisine.
