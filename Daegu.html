Welcome to Daegu

Daegu is known for its vibrant traditional markets, modern festivals, and historical sites. Visit the iconic Apsan Park and Dongseong-ro Street for shopping and dining.

Top Attractions in Daegu

Apsan Park

One of Daegu's most popular outdoor spots, Apsan Park offers stunning views of the city from the mountain peak. It's a great place for hiking, picnicking, and even taking a cable car to the top.

Seomun Market

Explore one of South Korea’s oldest markets. Seomun Market is famous for its textiles, traditional Korean food, and vibrant atmosphere. Be sure to try the famous kalguksu (hand-cut noodles) while you're there.

E-World & 83 Tower

For family-friendly fun, head to E-World, a theme park with roller coasters, parades, and seasonal festivals. Don’t forget to visit the 83 Tower for panoramic views of Daegu.

Daegu's Best Local Dishes

Makchang (막창)

Daegu is famous for its grilled beef or pork intestines, known as "makchang." Served with a dipping sauce, it's a must-try for adventurous eaters.

Daegu Jjim galbi (찜갈비)

This dish consists of braised beef ribs, simmered with vegetables in a rich soy-based sauce. It’s a favorite among locals and tourists alike.

Travel Tips for Daegu

  • Best Time to Visit: Spring (March-May) and autumn (September-November) offer the most pleasant weather for exploring the city.
  • Transportation: Daegu has an efficient subway system and buses, making it easy to get around. Taxis are also affordable and widely available.
  • Festivals: Don't miss the Daegu Chimac Festival, where you can enjoy Korea’s favorite combo: fried chicken and beer.
